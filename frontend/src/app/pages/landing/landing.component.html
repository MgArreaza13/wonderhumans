ABOUT US

WonderHumans was a project that came out from the idea of our founder fixing an existing need. already had, we decided to put all our tech to a Wonderful use. WonderHumans exists to reveal the stories and needs of those (in need/homeless) we pass every day, and provide an effective, cashless way to respond.

  • Homeless will leverage from the power of small donations to provide real help to people that need it the most
  • Street homeless neighbors with (immediate short-term) needs and helping them re-enter society thru freelance jobs in their line of work/talent
  • Through the website, QR code, Beacons you can contribute to their basic Through Bluetooth beacons and profiles, you can even see if there’s anyone who is in need of something nearby.
  • You can view profiles, Biography / Portfolio (skills) / Stats (donation tracking) / List of needs.
  • Opening the website and scanning a QR code or going to “Around Me” screen showing any homeless nearby. Scanning their QR code also detects the small beacon carried by each Homeless client that emits a Bluetooth signal over a radius.
  • By donating/scanning to a specific person you can now view the profiles and registries of all WonderHuman clients, regardless of if there are any around you.
  • You can also donate/hire again to one of your past homeless without the need of having them in front of you.
  • By scanning or Clicking on any profile it will provide more information about the person, including how he or she became homeless, their short-term/long-term employment and housing goals, and what they have requested from WonderHuman donors. Clicking on “Donate now” brings you to the website wallet, which allows you to donate /hire directly to the homeless.

Our Mission & Vision

To provide a technology platform that empowers WonderHumans all over the world to help end homelessness through financial donations and employment opportunities.

WHY?

To provide a technology platform that allows community members to make monetary contributions as gifts or in exchange for services rendered that meet the skill set and abilities of an individual. By providing this platform, we can all be WonderHumans and make a positive impact worldwide.

Together we can all be Wonder Humans.

HOW?

The social ecosystem provides its users a means to identify those in need, as well as a way to interact daily and contribute as often as desired. Our WonderHumans help in many ways, from helping to geo-tag those in need, creating profiles that highlight skill sets and tell their stories, to making financial contributions that help those in need get back on their feet and into more stable living conditions.
