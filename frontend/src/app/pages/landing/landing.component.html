Wonder Humans The Product

Wonder Humans is a technology platform designed to provide easy access to the homeless population within local communities. The platform allows its users to identify, interact with and offer support to those in need. The Wonder Humans beta is rolling out in Miami as its first market, to test and perfect the platform, allowing for seamless duplication in every city.

Our Mission

To provide a technology platform that empowers WonderHumans all over the world to help end homelessness through financial donations and employment opportunities.

WHY?

To provide a technology platform that allows community members to make monetary contributions as gifts or in exchange for services rendered that meet the skill set and abilities of an individual in need. Empower anyone that wants to help to be a Wonder Humans and make a positive impact worldwide.

Together we can all be Wonder Humans.

Provide support, resources and tools the homeless need in order to change their current situation and reach their full potential as they re-enter society.

HOW?

The Wonder Humans platform provides its users a means to identify those in need, as well as a way to interact daily and contribute as often as desired. Our Wonder Humans help in many ways, from helping to geo-tag those in need, creating profiles that highlight skill sets and tell their stories, to making financial contributions to help homeless community members get back on their feet and into more stable living conditions.

Kindness

We empower Wonder Humans to give, impact, and change the lives of those in need, with the only expectation being a smile in return.

Determination

We work together with all supporters and participants in order to succeed and carry out the Wonder Humans mission.

Responsibility

We create an impact and change lives by working together and following a proven tech-driven execution strategy paired with caring human touch.
